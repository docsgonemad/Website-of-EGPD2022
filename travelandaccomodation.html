Host University of RWTH Aachen

The EASYGO team is proud to host EGPD 2022 in one of our main collaborating universities : RWTH Aachen University. RWTH Aachen is known for its world-class education in natural sciences and engineering. Stimulating ideas of cutting-edge technology and engineering are roaming around the entire tri-border region of Germany, Belgium and the Netherlands, providing the scientific network for young researchers and engineers of future. An innovative science community is evolving by encouraging networking with the world’s leading research and industry partners. Aachen, known as an “Europastadt” (City of Europe), is Germany’s westernmost major city. Three nations with three languages converge at its boundaries putting Aachen at the center of a dynamic, cross-border region. More than 9,500 international RWTH students from over 135 countries convey the scientific center of Aachen an international flair.

How to reach campus ?

This year, EGPD will be hosted by RWTH Aachen University between the days of 27th to 29th of April 2022 at the Ford-Saal conference room of C-Building at Templergraben 57, Aachen.
